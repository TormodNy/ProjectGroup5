3. Tester Audience

To reach testers that are similar to the websites intended audience we contacted. The age range of the target audience is pretty wide, therefore we tried reaching out to audienes of all age groups. We also asked whether the tester would be interested in buying a picture from the website to determine if we had managed to reach out to the target audience. We tested the website live with our first tester, and had him give us live feedback while also answering our Google Form. As the feedback was live, we got to working on the bugs he found right away.
