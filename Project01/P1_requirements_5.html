Our Client

Client Name: Henrik Giil Liisberg

Henrik is a twenty year old hobby-photographer from Norway currently residing in Trondheim.

Goals

The purpose of the website is to display a gallery of images that Henrik has taken, and make it possible to buy various prints of these images. We want to make it possible to contact the photographer, see the prizes and purchase pictures.

Target Audience

The target audience of the webpage are people who are looking for quality photographs on quality prints. Our audience is not the ordinary IKEA-goer, but someone who wants to pay way too much to make their living room a tiny bit less boring.

Content

  • Home

    Home screen with some pictures and description of the webpage with links to the different pages.

  • Gallery

    A gallery which displays the pictures, possibly divided into subsections based on where the pictures were taken.

  • Shop

    An online store where the user can choose a picture you want to buy a print of.

  • Prize Guidelines

    A table or another way of displaying the prizes of the depending on the quality and sizes of the prints.

  • Contact

    Contact information or integrated contact field where the user can ask the photographer any questions they might have.

  • About

    Information about the photographer.

Client Preferences

  • Clean, minimalistic front-page integrated with photos from the client.
  • Usage of the font Roboto (best font ever).
